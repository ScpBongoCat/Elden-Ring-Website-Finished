Who created Elden Ring

Elding Ring is an fantasy adventure game created by FromSoftware and and published by BANDAI NAMCO directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki on devices such as the playstation, Xbox, and PC.

What is the game about?

Elden Ring follows the story of a tarnished warrior awakening in The Lands Between, a land spilling with blood and war from the shattering of its great Elden Ring by the hands of Queen Marika, in which it is your duty to guide this warrior through The Lands Between defeating great legends, powerful warriors, and accursed rulers to reassemble the pieces of the shattered Elden Ring

The Story

The first area which a tarnished arrive in in the lands between are the Sprawling fields of Limgrave, a land of never seen before creatures, giants, dragons, and most importantly ruled by the wretched Godrick the Grafted, a pitiful excuse for a Lord gains his powers by grafting different body parts onto himself but before reaching his castle another foe awaits...

Margit the Fell Omen

Protecting the castle of Godrick the Grafted is Margit the Fell Omen, a child of royal blood cast out because of his omen curse, and here to stop any tarnished who wants to challenge the ruler of Limgrave

Godrick the Grafted

After defeating Margit you may now navigate Stormveil Castle, a place riddled with traps, enemies, and the lord of all that is golden, Godrick, Once you go through the castle you may meet the shardbearer of Limgrave, Godrick the Grafted during the fierce battle versus the amalgamated mess of grafted limbs which he will use his battleaxe to swing at you and demenish your healthbar, after Godrick has lost half his health he will cut off his own arm and graft the head of a mighty dragon onto it for the last phase of the fight you wont forget as he spews dragonfire around the arena.

The Roundtable Hold

After this victory versus Godrick you will be able to access the meeting place of all noteable Tarnished warriors, The Roundtable Hold, this place serves as a resting place and shop for any tarnished that has accomplished large feats in the Lands Between, including you now,you will be greeted by a few of its residents such as Gideon Ofnir, Corhyn, Diallos, D Hunter of the Dead, Master Hewg, and many other characters, use this area as a a shop to refresh after battles or a long adventure.

Liurnia of the Lakes

After exploring The Roundtable Hold you may continue to the next area of the game Liurnia of the Lakes which houses many interesting areas such as the frenzyflamed village, Caria Manor and the most important Raya Lucaria, school of magic. To enter this area you will need to find a key that can be located west of the academy protected by a glintstone dragon, slay this beast and you will be on your way to Raya Lucaria

Rennala Queen of the Full Moon

After aquiring the glintstone key you may now enter Raya Lucaria Academy home of the second shardbearer Rennala Queen of the Full Moon. naviagting your way through may be difficult with the number of illusionary walls and magic weilding enemies there are difficult but you can slowly make your way through defeating the Red Wolf of Radagon along the way and eventually you will come face to face with Queen Rennala. In the first phase of this fight you will need to diminish the number of Rennala's children focusing down the ones carrying golden glowsto break her shield. Do this a couple time and Queen Rennala will move onto here second stage. In the nxt stage Rennala will fire out a multitude of powerful magical attacks aswell as summoning enemy spirits like the giants or even a dragon. Rennala is incredibly weak in her health and can be taken down in a very short time if powerful enough and the battle will be yours

Caelid

One of the next regions you can travel to in elden ring is the deserted rotten lands of Caelid, a region full of death and the next shardbearer on your journey Starscourge Radahn. Caelid has many areas to explore such as Dragonbarrow, home of the modern age dragons, or the destroyed Sellia Town of Sorcery, or you can travel to Radahns castle to celebrate a certain festival...

Starscourge Radahn

The Radahn Festival, a triumphant tradition gathering champions from around the Lands between to take a shot at taking down the demigod Radahn, when you start the festival there will be multiple summon signs around for the many different characters to join the festival or you could take the difficult route and not summon any, but that is your choice to make. Not depending on either you choose to summon or not this will be a difficult battle, Radahn boasts a massive health bar with strong fast attacks to boot so taking him down will not be easy. When you bring his health to half by dodging the massive sword swings and gravity attacks Radahn will enter phase two jumping into the atmosphere, Radahn comes crashing down in ball of scorching fire has his attacks become more violent and desperate but as you widdle the health down you can finally defeat him to claim the 3rd shard of the elden ring.

Altus Plateau

The next area is the home of the Erdtree itself sprawling golden lands tainted by the actions of warfare, your sure to discover many things here such as the Windmill Village and the most important area Leyndell Royal Capital.

Leyndell Royal Capital

Leyndell Royal Capital home of the Erdtree which houses Marika and the Elden Ring. To enter you first need to travel around the side to the Draconic Tree Sentinel guarding the door, defeat him and continue on, the 2nd requirement is needing to have 2 Great Runes from the shardbearers (Refer to previous bosses for info) and then your allowed to enter the capitol. Leyndell is full of strong enemies, hidden paths, and many items to collect. Travelling through will be hard but once you get to the first boss of this area it is smooth sailing. Eventually you will reach the first boss of the area Godfrey, the First Elden Lord, or atleast a ghost of him defeat him and continue the short walk to the main boss of Leyndell

Morgott, The Omen King

As the cutscene starts you can see Margit climb down the stairs and reveals his true indentity as the true king of Leyndell, Morgott the Omen King this boss fight is a difficult one as Morgott has his Brutal fast attacks from before aswell as using holy damaging spells and a weapon to inflict bleed but aslong as you learn the attack patterns and make sure to dodge you will be able to defeat him opening up the path to the Erdtree, but unfortunatly the Erdtree's entrance is blocked by a wall of thorns and after asking around you will be tasked with burning the Erdtree with the Forge of the Giants and the Mark of Eternal Death to open the Erdtree once and for all and too rebuild the Elden Ring

Volcano Manor

Before travelling to the mountaintop theres 1 last shardbearer to find located in Volcano Manor hidden among the depths of the volcano. To get there you will to climb the volcano west of the Altus Plateau then you will need to enter the Manor and talk to Lady Tanith, master of the Manor. From here you have two options. You can either Travel through the mini-dungeon of Volcano manor or you can fufill the requests of Lady Tanith to finally be able to face Rykard Lord of Blasphemy

Rykard Lord of Blasphemy

Entering into the chamber you can see a Figure of a giant snake in the distance, grab the spear weapon from the side and prepare for one of the most cinematic fights in the game. As you approach the snake will wake up and attack you with a flurry of moves from tail to head you will be dgodging and dishing out damage with the hunter spear constantly, and after you lower the serpents health Rykard Lord Of Blasphemy will show himself, Rykard attached to the back of the serpent reveals himself and prepares for battle, in this state he will do amny attacks with his sword, fire spells, and the previous serpent attacks it wont be an easy fight but the beast will eventually go down for you to claim the 4th Great Rune

Mountaintop of the Giants

From Leyndell you can enter the next area of the game that being the Mountaintop of the Giants trough an elevator which can be found in Leyndell, The Mountaintop of the Giants is a vaste area full of powerful enemies and some of the best weapons in the game such as the Rivers Of Blood. Travel up and around the mountaintop to reach the next point of progression in the game.

Fire Giant

Following up the mountain you will reach another golden boss gate to face the Fire Giant, this boss is among the largest and tankiest in the game so using percentage status effects like blackflame or bleed is advised, this bosses weak spot can be spotted by the scars already on his body located on his leg underneath a little bit of protection, break the protection and dodge his fire attacks and you wil be able to go to phase 2 to fight. During phase 2 of this fight the Fire Giant will use many fire realted attacks that you must dodge, these do deal a ton of damage so you will need to react fast. Since in this phase Fire Giant has gotten rid of his injured leg there are only 3 weak spots 2 on either arm and his eye when it opens so make sure you take that advantage whenever possible and before you know iot Fire Giant will be defeated.

Concencrated Snowfield

Before heading up to the forge you will want to travel to the Concencrated Snowfield. By gathering 2 medallion pieces located in a castle at the top of Mountaintop Of the Giants and the 2nd piece located in Liurnia you can use the secret medallion to travel to the Snowfield, this area is very desolate having only a few areas to go, one of the mst important being Ordina the Liturgical Town here you will find an area where you have to light torches while being shot by arrows and being tracked down by invisible black knife assassins to open p a path to a fabled Haligtree

The Haligtree

The Haligtree is one of the games most difficult areas housing some of the most difficult enemies and plenty of scarlet rot to boot. The trravel down the tree is very dificult but rewarding as there are many items and runes to aquire. A few tips for before entering is aquiring the ability to get rid of rot (either through spells like flame cleanse me, or rejuvinating boluses) aswell as having ample levels (level 120-150 is good for this area). After climbing down the tree you better be prepared for the most difficult boss in this game

Malenia Blade of Miquella

Highly regarded as Elden Rings most difficult boss Malenia Blade of Miquella, with fast attacks and having the ablity to heal whenever she hits you, plus her dreaded Waterfowl dance this boss is nothing to be toying around with. A great way of dealing with her is with a multitude of status effect. She is both weak too bleed and frost effects and can have her waterfowl dance cancelled mid attack. This boss may be difficult but with enough perseverence and preperation you can do it, and dont be ashamed if you cant beat her there are plenty of people ready to summon who have studied her moveset and wanting to help you during the fight.

Mohgwyn Palace

In the Consencrated Snowfield theres a secret entrance to the Mohgwyn Palace, hiding place of the shardbearer Mohg Lord of Blood. This area is one of the most difficult in the game having many enemies which inflict bleed and attack fast. Following up the stairs you can dodge many undead blood enemies and the Sanguine Nobles in the caves to reach an elevator up to the Mausoleum where you will find Mohg Lord of blood along with a dead Miquella

Mohg Lord of Blood

This shardbearer uses the power of the Formless Mother to cast blood incantations aswell as using his trident to stab and slash everything in an area, a great way to avoid these attacks is to stay far away, using ranged spells can be a great asset in this fight. Once Mohg has half hp he will use a certain atack building up levels of blood loss instantly and killing you if not dealt with. Two ways of contering this include just spamming your healing potions or aquiring the bloody crystal tear from the Second Church of Marika. in his second phase his attacks will become more frantic and powerful just continue dodging and you should be able to aquire another Great Rune.

Crumbling Farum Azula

After exploration you can now travel to the Forge of the Giants, make your way around the left side, sit at the site of grace, watch a quick cutscene and off to Crumbling Farum Azula you go. this area is infested with the dragons of the old age aswell as users of ancient dragon magic. To traverse this area you must hop between different islands of Farum Azula dodging and fighting your way through until eventually reaching the first boss of the area, The Godskin duo. After defeating the duo and travelling up the area and defeating a draconic tree sentinel you can enter the arena to the protector of the Mark of Eternal Death

Dragon Lord Placidusax

But before you do you can follow a simple path down and lay down on an open grave. As time stops and rewinds you come face to face with the true first owner of the Elden Ring, Dragon Lord Placidusax. This king of all ancient dragons attacks using lightning and different swipes and bites from the dragon will sure to keep you moving. The dragon after receiving a substancial amount of damage will start swooping down from the skies, these should be avoided as they deal quite a bit of damage. after getting him down more he will use Placidusax's Fury, a beam of pure fire. Dodging this sould be priority number 1 as getting hit by it is pretty much instant death, and after a long battle the Dragon Lord will fall.

Beast Clergyman

Entering the arena from before the foe ahead of you is the Beast Clergyman protector of the Mark of Eternal Death with a quick flurry of physical based attacks that can dish out lots of damage in a very short amount of time... make sure you learn the attack patterns and you can lower his hp in where a cutscene will play...

Maliketh the Black Blade

Stabbing the rune keeping the Mark of Eternal Death at bay the beast transforms into Maliketh the Black Blade. This boss os fast, and paired with the black flame inflicting attacks this is not an easy battle, and with his defeat you can claim the Mark of Eternal Death and burn the Erdtree.

Leyndell Capital of Ash

Being teleported back to Leyndell we can witness it covered in ash as the Erdtree burns above travelling back through the capital you travel to the room where you fought the ghost of Godfrey before and witness an old friend

Sir Gideon Ofnir the All Knowing

Sir Gideon Ofnir the All Knowing learning knowledge about Queen Marika herself forbids any tarnished from entering the Erdtree challenging you to a final battle to stop you from getting to the Erdtree, Gideon uses every spell you can learn from the remembrance bosses drop and bases which ones he can use off the bosses you have defeated already. defeating every boss up to this point will make this a difficult fight but is fairly easy if you've done some pvp up to this point, just stay calm, dodge and deal as much damage as possible as his healthbar is quite low and before you know it the path back up to the Ertdree will be open.

Godfrey, First Elden Lord

Travelling through the golden gate awaits Godfrey, the First Elden Lord holding the corpse of Morgott, his dead son, in this final battle all the stops come out as Godfrey protects the Erdtree and his heritage. Godfrey will attack using his axe and will hit hard with slow attacks and stomps dodging these will be a necessity, but before long you will widdle down the hp bar...

Hoarah Loux, Warrior

Being close to his final straw Godfrey breaks the neck of his dear lion spirit releasing his inner rage from the time of the badlands, going back to his past he transforms into his true form, Hoarah Loux, Warrior. This phase is completly different with a flurry of quick grabs and punches he is sure to keep you on your toes but before long the Warrior will fall leaving you with the burned path to the Erdtree.

Radagon of the Golden Order

Entering the Golden Fog you come across Marika hanging on the sigil of the Elden Ring for her crimes of smashing it, as the sigil cracks and Marika falls down we witness as she changes form into Radagon, husband of Marika and Ruler of the Golden Order. As he picks up the hammer used to smash the Elden Ring he starts his attack with a flurry of holy and physical based attacks, This boss has many difficult attacjks to dodge but it nt very difficult to beat nonetheless and once he falls the true battle begins.

Elden Beast

Out of the body of Marika comes a hand and then the body of the Great Elden Beast, sensing that you wont be a pawn to its cause the outer Gods have sent a beast to end you before aquiring the Elden Ring, With ist large healthbar and various Holy based attacks this boss serves as the final challenge of the game stopping you from repairing the Elden Ring. With your best gear in hand and the journey behind you slay the Elden Beast.

Final

With the Elden Beast slain you can now Repair the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord
